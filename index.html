Who We Are

WhyKnot is a small, black-owned business that specializes in all-natural hair and body care products. We cater to hair of all textures and types; whether yours is natural, processed, color-treated, or worn in a protective style, you can rest assured we have something just for you.

Shipping and Delivery

We're so excited that you decided to shop with us, and we're committed to making sure that all of our customers receive their orders in a timely manner. That being said, while we would love for our turnaround to be as expiditious Amazon's, we're still a small business. We make our products in small batches to ensure that they haven't spent months on the shelf by the time they're delivered. Consequentially, most orders will be packaged and shipped within five to seven business days. We greatly appreciate your business and patience, and thank you from the bottom of our hearts.
